Projects

Grob-103 Aerodynamics Analysis

The project aimed to visuallize pressure distribution around Grob-103 glider which is a sub-sonic low-alitude aircraft. The simulated environment were under the specified condition of the aircraft's flight altitude and speed. The simulation result was that pressure is the most extreme near the leading edges of the wings and the static pressure difference generates lift on the surface area of the wings. Technologies used were Solidworks and Ansys Fluent.
